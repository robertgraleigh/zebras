Barriers to Digital Inclusion & Equity

  • Lack of access to laptops at school and home

    6 out of 10 low-income, minority, and foster youth lack access to laptops at home.

  • Lack of access to high-speed internet

    70% of low-income, minority, and foster youth served by Log Camp do not have broadband or wireless internet access at home.

  • Lack of access curriculum, tools, resources & tools

    Students served by Log Camp do not have access to curriculum and resources wealthier students possess.
